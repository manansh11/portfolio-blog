---
title: "Give and Recieve"
date: "2025-05-03"
description: "How to stay in the flow of life"
---

Life is constant exchange.

Trees exhale oxygen; we return carbon dioxide. 

Flowers pour nectar into visiting bees; bees carry their pollen onward. 

When your purpose on this planet feels hazy, pause and notice what you’re giving—and what you’re receiving—right now.
